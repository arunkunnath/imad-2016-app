Mobile Revolution

The mobile phones have made revolutionary changes in the day to day activities of the people as a whole. It has become the part and parcel of the daily life. In future, mobile will become more relevant for all activities and it will become a tool between the individual and outside world. We have replaced watches, alarms, letters, instant communication like telegrams, video viewing, photography and communication to every part of the world with in seconds. It is now used for banking activities and that area again will have more innovation in future. There will be situation that you can do all your work with your fingertips that is mobile.
